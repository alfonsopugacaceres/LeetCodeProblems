Palindrome Problems:

	-A Palindrome is a word that when reversed gived the same word

	Example: racecar

	-A key component of these problems revolves around finding eitherunique or max length palindromes
	-For a palindrome to keep being a palindrome we have to add the same letter to both ends of the word. 

	Example: dracecard, udracecardu
